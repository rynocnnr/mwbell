At Michael W. Bell, D.D.S., we accept cash, personal checks, and all major credit cards. Your insurance co-pay will be due at the time of your visit. Any amount not covered by the patient’s dental insurance will be billed to the patient.

We are also proud to offer third party financing through CareCredit. If you desire to apply for this service, contact us and we will assist you as needed.

Your smile is as important to us as it is to you. Our team will work with your dental insurance company to help you achieve the dental results you need and desire. You are ultimately the responsible party with regard to your bill. To assist you, we file the claim on your behalf and accept payment from your insurance company as well. We’re well versed in the latest coverage trends, and are more than happy to discuss the particulars of your plan with you prior to treatment.
