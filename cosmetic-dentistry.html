Cosmetic Dentistry

Find Your Beautiful Smile

There are many ways cosmetic dentistry can heal or enhance your smile. Cosmetic dentistry can correct spaces between your teeth, stained or discolored teeth, chipped teeth, broken or missing teeth and “gummy” smiles. We will create a customized treatment plan for you based on your goals, age and financial comfort level. Contact us today and you can look great and have a healthy smile.

Porcelain Veneers

Custom made, thin strips of porcelain are adhered to the fronts of existing teeth to create a natural, beautiful smile. Crooked, discolored, chipped teeth and gaps are a thing of the past. Get the beauty and self-confidence you desire.

Tray Whitening

A beautiful, white smile is a home whitening treatment away. Our at-home treatment includes a customized whitening tray, and a prescription whitening gel. Our team will give you detailed instructions about how long you should wear the tray each day.

Lumineers

Lumineers by Cerinate is a painless procedure that gives you a straighter, whiter, more beautiful smile. These contact lens-thin "smile shapers" shape your teeth without harming your natural tooth structure, giving you a naturally beautiful smile.

Porcelain Bridges

Missing teeth due to gum disease, injury, decay or simply wear and tear don’t have to be an issue. A bridge can be used to correct the gap created by a missing tooth or missing teeth.

Porcelain Crowns

Tooth discoloration, chipping and cracking are easily repaired with a porcelain crown. After some tooth preparation, crowns are specifically fitted to restore functionality and enhance the appearance of your smile.

Tooth Colored Fillings

Having a cavity doesn't mean you have to get a silver filling. We can create a composite resin, tooth-colored filling that will restore your beautiful smile.

Bonding

Using a tooth-colored material adhered to the surface of the tooth we can correct chipped, broken, spaced and discolored teeth. We will maintain the natural structure and color of the tooth and sculpt the resin to create natural looking teeth.

Gum Contouring

Do you see a lot of your gum when you smile? This condition is treatable with advanced cosmetic procedures. We can re-contour your gum and add veneers to give you a beautiful, natural-looking smile.
