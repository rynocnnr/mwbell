Dental Technology

Innovative Dentistry

Dental visits do not have to be a source of stress. At our dental office, your comfort and safety are the top priority for our caring dental team. We use the latest technology, equipment and techniques to ensure satisfaction and continued oral health.

Digital X-Rays

Digital X-Ray technology can reduce exposure by as much as 90%. Images appear in under five seconds and can be enlarged for easier more detailed viewing.

Micro-Dentistry

Early cavity detection equals a less intrusive treatment. The DIAGNOdent® allows us to find previously undetectable cavities in their earliest stages. This small, portable laser unit allows us to detect and treat cavities the size of a pinhole.

Cosmetic Dentistry Planning

Using clinical information and computer imaging, we will help guide you to a plan for final treatment. Dr. Bell has had extensive training and experience and we assure you that the results will be beautiful!

Intraoral Camera

With this tiny camera we can show you the current conditions inside your mouth. The images are displayed on a video screen for educational purposes. Viewing this is always optional. Ask Dr. Bell or your hygienist if you are interested.

Dental Education System

The Caesy™ Educational System is the most advanced dental education system available. If you want to see how a procedure is done, we can play a short video illustration to help you understand.

Sedation Dentistry

Our office is designed with your comfort and relaxation in mind. We offer sedation dentistry for a very relaxing and anxiety-free experience. We offer oral and gas sedation depending on the treatment required. Ask for it on your next visit.
